NIKKI PAIGE RECORDS WITH SCOTT STORCH AND RANCE AT MFRECORDZ'S SUPERYACHT STUDIO

Rising Indie Pop Artist Takes Her Music to Sea in the Most Extraordinary Recording Environment in the World

LOS ANGELES, CA — MFRecordz announces that rising indie pop artist Nikki Paige is currently recording new music with legendary producer Scott Storch and acclaimed songwriter and producer Rance at MFRecordz's one-of-a-kind superyacht recording studio — a fully professional, world-class studio built aboard a luxury superyacht off the coast of Los Angeles.

The sessions mark a pivotal moment in Nikki Paige's career, bringing together one of music's most iconic production teams inside a studio unlike anything else in the industry. Scott Storch, whose production credits span Beyoncé, Dr. Dre, Chris Brown, 50 Cent, and countless others, and Rance (1500 or Nothin — Beyoncé, Kanye West, Jay-Z, Kendrick Lamar, Mary J. Blige), whose work has shaped some of the most important records of the modern era, are collaborating with Nikki on what promises to be a landmark project.

"There is no studio in the world like this one," said Nikki Paige. "Recording on the water with Scott and Rance — it changes how music feels. You can hear it in every take."

MFRecordz's superyacht studio is the flagship facility of the independent label's artist development operation. Featuring a full professional DAW setup, world-class monitoring, and an environment designed to unlock creative freedom, the studio has become a destination for artists and producers seeking something beyond the conventional.

The superyacht studio sessions follow a breakout period for Nikki Paige, who has built an audience of over 60,000 Spotify followers through her signature blend of indie pop artistry and genre-defying production. Her catalog includes standout tracks "Dot Dot Dot," "IMPORTANT," "Rain," and "Is This What You Wanted?" — each showcasing the vocal range and emotional depth that has drawn industry attention to the artist.

"Nikki Paige is the real thing — the kind of artist you don't have to manufacture," said Rance (1500 or Nothin). "The moment she walked into this studio, we knew we were making something special. The superyacht, the energy, Scott — everything aligned. This music is going to hit different."

"I've worked with some of the greatest artists in the world, and Nikki belongs in that conversation," said Scott Storch. "There's something about recording on the water that strips everything down to what matters — the song, the voice, the feeling. Nikki has all three. This record is going to turn heads."

New music from the superyacht sessions is expected to be released later this year.
