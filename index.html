Our Vision

An Independent Powerhouse at Sea

MF Recordz is a collective and independent powerhouse — redefining what a recording studio can be. We've created the first-ever floating luxury recording studio and event hub on a 100-foot yacht, merging music creation, high-end lifestyle, and cultural experiences in a space that exists nowhere else on earth.


In partnership with Club Malibu, the MF Recordz Yacht Experience is where artists create without limits, brands activate at the highest level, and culture is made on the water.
